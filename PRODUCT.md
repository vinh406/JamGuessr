# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Friends and music enthusiasts who want to play real-time music guessing games using their Spotify library. They gather in rooms (in-person or remote), compete to identify songs, and share a social music experience.

## Product Purpose

A multiplayer song guessing game where players use their Spotify library to create or join game rooms, listen to previews, and compete to identify tracks. Points reward both accuracy and speed. The game is ephemeral — all state lives in memory during play, with only final results persisted.

## Brand Personality

Energetic, playful, music-first.

## Anti-references

Cluttered game UIs — flashy, noisy, over-designed interfaces with too much going on at once. The UI should stay clean and focused even during fast-paced gameplay. Music should be the hero, not decoration.

## Design Principles

- **Music-first energy** — the music preview is the focal point; the UI serves the listening and guessing, not the other way around.
- **Social clarity** — multiplayer interactions (who's ready, who answered, scores updating) are transparent and easy to read at a glance.
- **Playful but not noisy** — energetic and fun but never cluttered, flashy, or overwhelming.
- **Speed rewards clarity** — fast-paced rounds demand clear, unambiguous UI state. Every element answers "what do I do now?" in under a second.
- **Consistent dark confidence** — the dark theme creates a focused, theater-like environment for listening, like a dim room where the music is the spotlight.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

No specific WCAG level required, but the interface should work well for all players. Clear contrast, predictable layouts, and responsive feedback are baseline expectations.
